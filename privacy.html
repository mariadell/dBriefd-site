Privacy Policy

dBriefd ("we," "our") operates the Briefd mobile app and website. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data we collect

Account data: email, password (hashed), topic preferences, delivery settings. Usage data: app opens, briefing views, playback events. Device data: device type, OS version, app version. Payment data is processed by Apple/Google; we do not store card numbers.

How we use it

To deliver your daily briefings, personalize content, process subscriptions, improve the app, and support you. We do not sell your data. We do not display ads.

Third parties

We use: Apple (App Store, payments, sign-in); Google (Play Store, payments); hosting and analytics providers. We may share data only as necessary to operate the service or when required by law.

Data retention

Account data: until you delete your account. Usage data: anonymized or deleted within 24 months. Payment records: retained for legal/tax reasons.

Your rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your data. In the app: Settings → Account. Or request via contact@dbriefd.app. EU/UK users: you may withdraw consent, object, or lodge a complaint with your privacy regulator.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS, TLS) and secure storage. We do not sell or share your data for advertising.

Children

Briefd is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

International transfers

Data may be processed in the EU, US, or other jurisdictions. We use appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) where required.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via app or email. Continued use means acceptance.
