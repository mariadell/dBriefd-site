What is dBriefd

One daily briefing.
Tailored to you.

News today is noisy, endless, and overwhelming. You jump between apps, scroll too much, and still miss what matters. dBriefd replaces all of that with one daily briefing: short, clear summaries, only the topics you care about, delivered once per day. Stay informed without stress.

01 / CONFIGURE
Pick your world

Choose your topics and delivery time. Gaming, finance, books, health. Mix however you want. Your brief is yours alone.

02 / RECEIVE
Your brief arrives

We gather, filter, and summarize content into one daily update. Clean summaries. No clickbait. No algorithm games. Just signal.

03 / CONSUME
Read or listen

Turn your briefing into a podcast-style audio digest. Commute, gym, coffee. Consume your world on your terms, eyes free.

04 / DIG IN
Go deeper when you want

Every summary links back to the full article. Curious? One tap opens it in-app. No rabbit holes, no tab explosions.
